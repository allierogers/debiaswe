Representative Words

(Required for all options)

Input 1+ pairs of words that represent your area of potential two-category bias in the format:

category_1_word_1, category_2_word_1, category_1_word_2, category_2_word_2, .... , category_1_word_n, category_2_word_n

Example for gender: he, she, him, her, his, hers, mr., ms.

Words of Interest

Leave blank to use default set of professions

Debiasing Inputs

Definitional Words

More general form of the Representative Words input above. Can simply be two sets; each word does not need to have a corresponding partner to form a pair.

Specific Words NOT to Neutralize

These are pairs of words that should NOT be neutralized on our subspace of interest. For example, the relationship between brother and sister would lose meaning if it were neutralized on the gender subspace.

Equality Sets

These are pairs of words that SHOULD be neutralized on our subspace of interest. For example, one might want the relationship between doctor and nurse to be neutral on the gender subspace.
